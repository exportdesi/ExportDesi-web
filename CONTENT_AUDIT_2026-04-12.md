## 1. HomePage Audit

### Current State

**Title Tag:** "Structured Export Execution from India"  
**Hero Label:** "Export Execution"  
**Hero Headline:** "Structured Export Execution from India."  
**Primary CTA:** "Submit Your Requirement"  
**Secondary CTA:** "How We Work"

### Copywriting Analysis

#### Strengths
- Clear value proposition: "We do not manufacture. We qualify processors and manage the shipment."
- Strong differentiation: "Merchant export management, not a trading house"
- Specific process details: 4 criteria for processor assessment
- Trust markers: FSSAI, APEDA, Phytosanitary certificate mentioned

#### Weaknesses

**1. Headline is too generic**  
"Structured Export Execution from India" — What does "structured" mean? This is company-speak, not customer language.

**Recommended rewrites:**
- **Option A (Benefit-led):** "Export Indian Food Ingredients Without Managing Multiple Vendors"
- **Option B (Outcome-led):** "From Processor to Port: Single-Point Export Management for Indian Food Ingredients"
- **Option C (Direct):** "Your On-Ground Export Partner in India — Processor Qualification, Documentation, Shipment"

**Why:** The headline should answer "What do I get?" not "What are you?"

**2. CTA copy is weak**  
"Submit Your Requirement" sounds like filling out a form, not starting a relationship.

**Recommended rewrites:**
- **Option A:** "Get a Sourcing Quote"
- **Option B:** "Discuss Your Requirement"
- **Option C:** "Request Processor Availability"

**Why:** "Submit" implies work for them. "Get" or "Discuss" implies value for them.

**3. Definition block buried**  
The clearest value prop ("Export Desi is a merchant export management operation...") is in paragraph 3, not above the fold.

**Recommended fix:** Move the "What We Are" section closer to hero or integrate into hero subtitle.

**4. Missing social proof above the fold**  
Certificates are shown but not highlighted with credibility markers.

**Recommended addition:** Add specific numbers:
- "X+ shipments coordinated"
- "X countries served"
- "X% on-time documentation"

## 2. AboutPage Audit

### Current State

**Hero Title:** "Export Desi."  
**Hero Subtitle:** 3 lines explaining coordination role  
**Founder Section:** Rajiv Dudeja, established 2013, 10+ years experience  
**Team Section:** 3 members with roles

### Copywriting Analysis

#### Strengths
- Clear positioning: "Export management operation, not a trading house"
- Strong founder credibility: 2013 establishment, decade of prior experience
- Team roles clearly defined with specific responsibilities
- Geographic anchoring: Gurgaon, Bihar, Gujarat

#### Weaknesses

**1. Hero title is wasted space**  
"Export Desi." tells visitors nothing. This is prime real estate.

**Recommended rewrites:**
- **Option A:** "Your Partner for Indian Food Ingredient Exports"
- **Option B:** "10+ Years Connecting Indian Processors to Global Buyers"
- **Option C:** "Export Desi: Processor-Qualified Supply from India"

**Why:** The hero title should complement the subtitle, not repeat the company name.

**2. Founder section lacks proof points**  
"Established Export Desi in 2013" — but no evidence of scale or track record.

**Recommended additions:**
- "X+ successful shipments"
- "X countries served"
- "X+ active processor relationships"
- Specific past companies or achievements (if available)

**3. Team section missing credibility**  
No LinkedIn profiles, no photos mentioned, no years of experience.

**Recommended additions:**
- "X years in food export sourcing"
- "Previously with [Company]"
- "Specializes in [Product/Region]"

**4. Final CTA is generic**  
"Want to discuss a sourcing requirement?" — soft and non-committal.

**Recommended rewrites:**
- **Option A:** "Ready to Source from India? Get a Processor Quote in 48 Hours"
- **Option B:** "Start Your Sourcing Discussion — Response Within 48 Business Hours"
- **Option C:** "Verify Processor Availability for Your Requirement"

## Cross-Page Recommendations

### 1. Add Consistent Trust Markers

**All pages should show:**
- GST, IEC, APEDA, FSSAI, Spice Board, FIEO numbers
- "X+ years in export business"
- "X countries served"
- Response time commitment ("48 business hours")

### 2. Standardize CTA Language

**Current inconsistency:**
- HomePage: "Submit Your Requirement"
- TurmericPage: "Request a Quote" / "Request a Sample"
- AboutPage: "Submit Your Requirement"

**Recommended standardization:**
- Primary CTA: "Get a Sourcing Quote"
- Secondary CTA: "Request a Sample" (where applicable)
- Tertiary CTA: "How We Work" (for researchers)

### 3. Add Pricing to All Product Pages

**Turmeric page sets the standard** with price ranges. Add similar pricing to:
- Makhana page (by Suta grade)
- Dehydrated page (by product type)
- Future product pages

**Why:** B2B buyers expect pricing transparency. Even ranges reduce unqualified enquiries.

### 4. Improve Meta Descriptions with CTAs

**Pattern to apply:**
"[What you offer] + [Key differentiator] + [CTA/timeframe]"

Example: "Export Desi supplies GI-tagged Makhana from Bihar. FSSAI, APEDA certified. Get a quote within 48 hours."

### 5. Add Schema Markup Consistency

**Turmeric page has Product schema.** Ensure all product pages have:
- Product schema with offers
- AggregateOffer for multiple variants
- BreadcrumbList schema for navigation
- FAQPage schema for FAQ sections

## Content Quality Notes (Humanizer Principles)

While the `humanizer` skill installation didn't complete, the following AI-writing patterns were checked:

### What's Good
- ✅ Specific numbers throughout (curcumin %, moisture %, prices)
- ✅ Active voice: "We qualify processors" not "Processors are qualified"
- ✅ No buzzwords: "streamline", "optimize", "innovative" avoided
- ✅ Concrete details: State names, certification numbers, lead times

### What to Watch
- ⚠️ "Structure international B2B supply" — corporate jargon
- ⚠️ "Managed from Gurgaon" — passive construction
- ⚠️ "Comprehensive compliance pathway" — vague abstraction

**Rule of thumb:** If a human wouldn't say it in a conversation, rewrite it.
